## Account & Login

### How do I create an account?

1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
2. Choose your role (Landlord or Tenant)
3. Fill in your information
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

The reset link expires after 24 hours. If it expires, request a new one.

### Can I change my email address?

Yes! Go to **Settings** > **Profile** and update your email address. You'll need to verify the new email address.

### How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?

1. Go to **Settings** > **Security**
2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
4. Enter the 6-digit code
5. Save your backup codes in a secure location

### What if I lose access to my 2FA device?

Use one of your backup codes to log in, then disable and re-enable 2FA with a new device. If you don't have backup codes, contact support.

### Can I have both a landlord and tenant account?

Yes, but you'll need separate email addresses for each account. Each account type has different features and permissions.

### How do I delete my account?

Contact support at support@propertymanagement.com to request account deletion. Note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

## For Landlords

### How do I add a property?

1. Go to **Properties**
2. Click "Add Property"
3. Fill in property details
4. Upload photos
5. Save the property

### Can I manage multiple properties?

Yes! There's no limit to the number of properties you can manage. Add as many properties as you need.

### How do I invite a tenant?

1. Go to **Tenants**
2. Click "Invite Tenant"
3. Enter the tenant's email
4. Select the property
5. Send the invitation

The tenant will receive an email to create their account.

### How do I create a lease?

Use the Lease Creation Wizard:
1. Go to **Leases** > **Create Lease**
2. Select property and tenant
3. Set lease terms (dates, rent, deposits)
4. Add additional terms
5. Review and send for signature

### Can I use my own lease template?

Yes! You can upload custom lease templates or use our pre-built templates. Go to **Leases** > **Templates** to manage templates.

### How do I process rent payments?

Tenants pay online through the portal. Payments are automatically recorded. You can also manually record payments received outside the system.

### How do I handle maintenance requests?

1. View requests in **Maintenance**
2. Review request details and photos
3. Assign a vendor or handle it yourself
4. Update the status as work progresses
5. Mark as completed when done

### Can I assign vendors to maintenance requests?

Yes! Go to **Vendors** to add your service providers, then assign them to maintenance requests. Track their performance and ratings.

### How do I view financial reports?

Go to **Financial** > **Dashboard** to see:
- Revenue and expenses
- Occupancy rates
- Cash flow
- Property performance
- Custom reports

### Can I export financial data?

Yes! Most reports can be exported as PDF, Excel, or CSV files. Look for the "Export" button on report pages.

### How do I communicate with tenants?

Use the built-in messaging system:
1. Go to **Messages**
2. Click "New Message"
3. Select tenant(s)
4. Compose and send

You can also send bulk messages to multiple tenants at once.

### What happens when a lease expires?

The system sends automatic renewal reminders at 90, 60, and 30 days before expiration. You can send a renewal offer or let the lease expire naturally.

### How do I handle security deposit returns?

1. After lease termination, conduct move-out inspection
2. Go to lease detail page
3. Click "Process Security Deposit"
4. Document any deductions
5. Process refund
6. System generates itemized statement

### Can I set up automatic late fees?

Yes! Configure late fee rules in **Settings** > **Payment Settings**. The system will automatically apply late fees based on your rules.

Review your lease agreement for early termination terms. Contact your landlord to discuss options. There may be penalties or fees for breaking a lease early.

## Payments

### What payment methods are accepted?

- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Bank Account (ACH transfer)
- Check (manual entry by landlord)

### Are there fees for paying by credit card?

A small convenience fee may apply for credit card payments. ACH bank transfers typically have no fee. Check with your landlord for specific fee details.

### How long does it take for payments to process?

- **Credit/Debit Card**: Instant
- **Bank Account (ACH)**: 2-3 business days

### Can I schedule future payments?

Yes! When making a payment, you can select a future date. You can also set up auto-pay for recurring monthly payments.

### What if my payment fails?

You'll receive a notification if a payment fails. Check your payment method (sufficient funds, card not expired, correct information) and retry the payment.

### How do I get a receipt?

Receipts are automatically generated and emailed after each payment. You can also download receipts from your payment history at any time.

### Can I get a refund?

Refunds are processed by your landlord. Contact them to request a refund. If approved, refunds typically take 5-10 business days to appear in your account.

### What are late fees?

Late fees are charges applied when rent is paid after the due date plus grace period. Late fee amounts and grace periods are specified in your lease agreement.

### How can I avoid late fees?

- Set up auto-pay
- Set payment reminders
- Pay a few days early
- Keep sufficient funds in your account
- Contact landlord if you'll be late

### Can I make partial payments?

This depends on your landlord's policy. Contact your landlord to discuss payment arrangements if you cannot pay in full.

## Maintenance

### How quickly will my maintenance request be addressed?

Response times depend on priority:
- **Urgent**: Within 24 hours
- **High**: Within 2-3 days
- **Normal**: Within 5-7 days
- **Low**: Scheduled as convenient

### Can I track the status of my maintenance request?

Yes! Go to **Maintenance** to see all your requests and their current status. You'll also receive notifications when the status changes.

### What if the repair isn't done correctly?

Contact your landlord immediately. Reopen the maintenance request or submit a new one explaining the issue. Take photos if applicable.

### Can I do repairs myself?

Check your lease agreement. Most leases require landlord approval before making any repairs. Unauthorized repairs may affect your security deposit.

### What should I do if I have an emergency?

1. Call 911 if it's life-threatening
2. Call your landlord's emergency number immediately
3. Submit an urgent maintenance request
4. Follow your landlord's instructions
5. Document everything with photos

### How do I add photos to a maintenance request?

When creating or viewing a maintenance request, click "Add Photos" and upload up to 10 images. Photos help your landlord understand the issue better.

### Will I be charged for maintenance?

Normal wear and tear repairs are typically covered by your landlord. You may be charged for:
- Damage you caused
- Neglect or misuse
- Unauthorized modifications

Check your lease agreement for details.

### Do I need to be home for repairs?

Provide your availability when submitting the request. If you can't be home, discuss access arrangements with your landlord (spare key, lockbox, etc.).

### Can I request a specific vendor?

You can make suggestions, but your landlord chooses the vendor. They typically use licensed, insured professionals from their vendor network.

## Leases

### What's included in a lease agreement?

Typical lease terms include:
- Property address
- Tenant names
- Lease duration (start and end dates)
- Monthly rent amount
- Security deposit
- Payment due date
- Late fee policy
- Maintenance responsibilities
- Rules and regulations
- Pet policy
- Termination terms

### Can I negotiate lease terms?

Yes, you can discuss terms with your landlord before signing. Common negotiable items include rent amount, lease duration, and pet policies.

### How do I sign my lease electronically?

1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Click the link to review the lease
3. Read the entire document carefully
4. Click "Sign Document"
5. Draw or type your signature
6. Agree to terms and submit
7. You'll receive a signed copy via email

### Is an electronic signature legally binding?

Yes! Electronic signatures are legally binding in all 50 states under the ESIGN Act and UETA. They have the same legal weight as handwritten signatures.

### Can I make changes to the lease after signing?

Changes require an addendum signed by all parties. Contact your landlord to discuss any needed changes.

### What's the difference between a fixed-term and month-to-month lease?

- **Fixed-Term**: Specific start and end dates (e.g., 12 months). Provides stability and locked-in rent.
- **Month-to-Month**: Continues indefinitely until either party gives notice. Offers flexibility but rent can change with proper notice.

### How much notice do I need to give to move out?

Check your lease agreement. Typically:
- Fixed-term lease: 30-60 days before lease end
- Month-to-month: 30 days

### What happens if I don't give proper notice?

You may be responsible for rent until the end of the notice period or until a new tenant is found. Check your lease for specific terms.

### Can my landlord increase my rent?

For fixed-term leases, rent is typically locked in for the lease duration. For month-to-month or at renewal, landlords can increase rent with proper notice (usually 30-60 days, varies by state).

### What's a lease addendum?

An addendum is a document that adds to or modifies the original lease. Common addendums include pet addendums, parking agreements, or rule updates.

## Communication

### How do I contact my landlord?

Use the built-in messaging system:
1. Go to **Messages**
2. Click "New Message"
3. Compose and send

For emergencies, use the emergency phone number provided in your lease.

### How quickly should I expect a response?

Most landlords respond within 24-48 hours for non-urgent matters. Urgent issues should be addressed more quickly.

### Can I call my landlord?

Yes, but the messaging system is preferred for non-urgent matters as it creates a written record. Use phone calls for urgent issues or when immediate response is needed.

### What should I do if my landlord isn't responding?

1. Send a follow-up message
2. Try calling
3. Send a formal written notice if needed
4. Document all communication attempts
5. Contact local tenant rights organization if issue persists

### Can I communicate with other tenants?

The platform doesn't have a tenant-to-tenant messaging feature. Exchange contact information directly if you wish to communicate with neighbors.

### How do I report inappropriate communication?

Contact platform support at support@propertymanagement.com with details. Include screenshots if possible.

### Are my messages private?

Messages are private between you and your landlord. Platform administrators can access messages only for support or legal purposes.

## Technical Issues

### The website isn't loading. What should I do?

1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if issue persists

### I can't upload files. What's wrong?

Check that:
- File is under size limit (usually 10MB per file)
- File format is supported (PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG, etc.)
- You have a stable internet connection
- Your browser allows file uploads

### The app is running slowly. How can I fix it?

1. Close unnecessary browser tabs
2. Clear browser cache
3. Update your browser to the latest version
4. Check your internet speed
5. Try using a different browser

### I'm not receiving email notifications. Why?

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Add noreply@propertymanagement.com to your contacts
3. Verify your email address in settings
4. Check notification preferences
5. Contact support if emails still don't arrive

### Can I use the platform on my phone?

Yes! The platform is fully responsive and works on mobile devices. Access it through your mobile browser. There's currently no dedicated mobile app.

### Which browsers are supported?

Supported browsers:
- Google Chrome 90+
- Mozilla Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Microsoft Edge 90+

Internet Explorer is not supported.

### How do I report a bug?

Contact support at support@propertymanagement.com with:
- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Screenshots if applicable
- Browser and device information

### Is there a mobile app?

Currently, there's no dedicated mobile app, but the website is fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers.

## Security & Privacy

### Is my personal information secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard security measures:
- 256-bit SSL encryption
- Secure data centers
- Regular security audits
- Encrypted data storage
- Secure payment processing (PCI DSS compliant)

### Who can see my information?

- **Landlords**: Can see information of their tenants
- **Tenants**: Can only see their own information
- **Platform Administrators**: Have limited access for support purposes only

### How is my payment information stored?

Payment information is processed through Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. We don't store your full credit card numbers or bank account details.

### Can I delete my data?

Yes, you can request account deletion by contacting support. Note that some data may be retained for legal or accounting purposes.

### Do you sell my information?

No! We never sell your personal information to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details on how we use and protect your data.

### How do you use my data?

We use your data to:
- Provide platform services
- Process payments
- Send notifications
- Improve the platform
- Comply with legal requirements

See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

### What happens to my data if I delete my account?

Most personal data is deleted within 30 days. Some data may be retained for:
- Legal compliance
- Accounting records
- Fraud prevention

### How do I report a security concern?

Email security@propertymanagement.com immediately with details of the concern. We take security issues very seriously.
