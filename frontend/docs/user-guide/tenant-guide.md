## Finding Properties

### Property Search

1. Navigate to **Property Search** in the main menu
2. Browse available properties
3. Use filters to narrow your search:
   - **Location**: City, state, ZIP code, or neighborhood
   - **Property Type**: Apartment, house, condo, townhouse
   - **Price Range**: Set minimum and maximum rent
   - **Bedrooms**: Select number of bedrooms
   - **Bathrooms**: Select number of bathrooms
   - **Amenities**: Pool, gym, parking, pet-friendly, etc.
4. Sort results by:
   - Price (low to high / high to low)
   - Newest listings
   - Square footage
   - Bedrooms

### Viewing Property Details

Click on any property to see:
- **Photos**: Browse property images
- **Description**: Detailed property information
- **Amenities**: List of included amenities
- **Location**: Map and address
- **Rent Details**: Monthly rent, security deposit, pet deposit
- **Availability**: Move-in date
- **Property Features**: Bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, parking
- **Lease Terms**: Lease duration options
- **Contact Information**: Landlord or property manager details

### Saving Favorites

**Save Properties You Like:**
1. Click the heart icon on property card
2. Property is added to your favorites
3. Access favorites from **Property Search** > **Favorites**
4. Remove from favorites by clicking heart again

### Scheduling Tours

**Request a Property Tour:**
1. On property detail page, click **"Schedule Tour"**
2. Select preferred dates and times
3. Add any special requests or questions
4. Submit tour request
5. Landlord will respond with confirmation or alternative times
6. Receive confirmation email with tour details

## Submitting Applications

### Application Process

**Step 1: Start Application**
1. On property detail page, click **"Apply Now"**
2. Review application requirements
3. Click **"Begin Application"**

**Step 2: Personal Information**
- Full legal name
- Date of birth
- Social Security Number (encrypted and secure)
- Phone number
- Email address
- Current address
- How long at current address

**Step 3: Employment Information**
- Current employer name
- Employer address and phone
- Job title
- Employment start date
- Monthly gross income
- Supervisor name and contact

**Step 4: Additional Income** (if applicable)
- Source of additional income
- Monthly amount
- Documentation

**Step 5: Rental History**
- Previous landlord name and contact
- Previous address
- Dates of tenancy
- Reason for leaving
- Monthly rent paid

**Step 6: References**
- Personal references (2-3)
- Name, relationship, phone, email

**Step 7: Additional Information**
- Number of occupants
- Pets (type, breed, weight)
- Vehicles (make, model, license plate)
- Emergency contact

**Step 8: Documents**
Upload required documents:
- Photo ID (driver's license or passport)
- Proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns, bank statements)
- Employment verification letter
- Previous landlord reference letter (optional)
- Pet vaccination records (if applicable)

**Step 9: Background Check Authorization**
- Review and sign authorization form
- Consent to credit check
- Consent to background check
- Consent to rental history verification

**Step 10: Application Fee**
- Review application fee amount
- Pay application fee (credit card or ACH)
- Fee is non-refundable

**Step 11: Review and Submit**
- Review all information for accuracy
- Edit any section if needed
- Submit application
- Receive confirmation email

### Application Status

**Tracking Your Application:**
1. Go to **Dashboard** or **Applications**
2. View application status:
   - **Submitted**: Application received, under review
   - **Under Review**: Landlord is reviewing your application
   - **Additional Info Requested**: Landlord needs more information
   - **Approved**: Application approved, ready for lease
   - **Rejected**: Application not approved
3. Receive email notifications for status changes

**If Additional Information is Requested:**
1. You'll receive a notification
2. Go to your application
3. View requested information
4. Upload additional documents or provide information
5. Resubmit

### Application Tips

✅ **Be thorough** - Complete all fields accurately  
✅ **Be honest** - Provide truthful information  
✅ **Respond quickly** - Reply promptly to information requests  
✅ **Have documents ready** - Prepare required documents in advance  
✅ **Check for errors** - Review application before submitting  
✅ **Follow up** - Contact landlord if you don't hear back within a few days

## Viewing Lease Details

### Accessing Your Lease

1. Navigate to **My Lease** in the main menu
2. View your current lease information
3. Download lease agreement PDF
4. View lease history (if you've had multiple leases)

### Lease Information

**Your lease page shows:**
- **Property Details**: Address, unit number, property type
- **Lease Dates**: Start date, end date, lease duration
- **Rent Information**: Monthly rent, due date, late fee policy
- **Security Deposit**: Amount paid, refund conditions
- **Lease Terms**: Key terms and conditions
- **Landlord Information**: Name, contact details
- **Important Dates**: Rent due date, lease expiration, renewal deadline

### Lease Documents

**Available Documents:**
- Signed lease agreement
- Lease addendums
- Move-in inspection report
- Rules and regulations
- Parking agreement (if applicable)
- Pet addendum (if applicable)

**Downloading Documents:**
1. Go to **My Lease** > **Documents**
2. Click on document to view
3. Click **"Download"** to save PDF copy
4. Documents are also available in **Documents** section

### Lease Renewal

**Renewal Process:**
1. You'll receive renewal notifications:
   - 90 days before expiration
   - 60 days before expiration
   - 30 days before expiration
2. Review renewal offer from landlord
3. Options:
   - **Accept Renewal**: Accept terms and sign new lease
   - **Decline Renewal**: Notify landlord you're moving out
   - **Negotiate**: Request different terms
4. If accepting, sign new lease electronically
5. New lease becomes active on specified date

**Renewal Considerations:**
- Review any rent increases
- Check for changes in lease terms
- Consider your future plans
- Respond by the deadline
- Ask questions if anything is unclear

### Move-Out Process

**When Your Lease Ends:**
1. Provide proper notice (usually 30-60 days)
2. Schedule move-out inspection with landlord
3. Clean property thoroughly
4. Complete any necessary repairs
5. Return all keys and access devices
6. Provide forwarding address
7. Security deposit will be processed within 30 days (varies by state)

## Making Payments

### Payment Dashboard

Access payments at **Payments** in the main menu.

**Dashboard Shows:**
- Next payment due date and amount
- Payment status (paid, pending, overdue)
- Payment history
- Outstanding balance
- Available payment methods

### Making a Payment

**One-Time Payment:**
1. Go to **Payments**
2. Click **"Make Payment"**
3. Select payment type:
   - Rent payment
   - Late fee
   - Other charges
4. Enter amount (or use suggested amount)
5. Select payment method:
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Bank Account (ACH)
6. Review payment details
7. Click **"Submit Payment"**
8. Receive confirmation and receipt

**Payment Methods:**

**Credit/Debit Card:**
- Instant processing
- Small convenience fee may apply
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover accepted

**Bank Account (ACH):**
- No convenience fee
- Takes 2-3 business days to process
- Requires bank account and routing number

### Setting Up Auto-Pay

**Automatic Payments:**
1. Go to **Payments** > **Auto-Pay**
2. Click **"Set Up Auto-Pay"**
3. Select payment method
4. Choose payment date (e.g., 1st of month, 5th of month)
5. Confirm setup
6. You'll receive email confirmation before each automatic payment
7. Payments process automatically on the scheduled date

**Managing Auto-Pay:**
- View auto-pay status
- Update payment method
- Change payment date
- Pause auto-pay temporarily
- Cancel auto-pay

**Benefits of Auto-Pay:**
- Never miss a payment
- Avoid late fees
- Save time
- Build positive payment history

### Payment History

**Viewing Payment History:**
1. Go to **Payments** > **History**
2. View all past payments
3. Filter by:
   - Date range
   - Payment type
   - Payment method
   - Status
4. Search by transaction ID or amount

**Payment Details:**
Each payment shows:
- Payment date
- Amount paid
- Payment method
- Transaction ID
- Receipt number
- Status
- Associated lease

### Receipts

**Accessing Receipts:**
1. Go to payment history
2. Click on any payment
3. View receipt details
4. Download PDF receipt
5. Email receipt to yourself

**Receipt Information:**
- Payment date and time
- Amount paid
- Payment method
- Property address
- Lease information
- Transaction ID
- Receipt number
- Company information

### Late Payments

**If You Miss a Payment:**
1. You'll receive reminder notifications
2. Late fees may apply after grace period (check your lease)
3. Make payment as soon as possible
4. Contact landlord if you're having financial difficulties

**Payment Plans:**
If you're unable to pay in full:
1. Contact your landlord immediately
2. Explain your situation
3. Request a payment plan
4. If approved, follow the agreed-upon schedule
5. Keep communication open

### Payment Issues

**If Payment Fails:**
1. You'll receive notification of failed payment
2. Check your payment method:
   - Sufficient funds in bank account
   - Credit card not expired
   - Correct account information
3. Update payment method if needed
4. Retry payment
5. Contact support if issues persist

**Disputing Charges:**
1. Review charge details
2. Contact landlord to discuss
3. Provide documentation if applicable
4. Landlord will review and respond
5. Charges may be adjusted if error is found

## Submitting Maintenance Requests

### Creating a Maintenance Request

**Step 1: Start Request**
1. Navigate to **Maintenance** in the main menu
2. Click **"Submit Request"**

**Step 2: Describe the Issue**
- Select category:
  - Plumbing (leaks, clogs, water heater)
  - Electrical (outlets, lights, breakers)
  - HVAC (heating, cooling, ventilation)
  - Appliances (refrigerator, stove, dishwasher, washer/dryer)
  - Structural (walls, floors, ceilings)
  - Exterior (roof, siding, gutters)
  - Pest Control
  - Security (locks, doors, windows)
  - Other
- Write detailed description of the problem
- Specify location in unit (e.g., "kitchen sink", "master bedroom")

**Step 3: Set Priority**
- **Urgent**: Emergency (no heat/water, gas leak, security issue)
- **High**: Major inconvenience, needs immediate attention
- **Normal**: Standard repair, can wait a few days
- **Low**: Minor issue, cosmetic, can be scheduled

**Step 4: Add Photos**
- Take photos of the issue
- Upload up to 10 photos
- Photos help landlord understand the problem
- Drag and drop or click to upload

**Step 5: Provide Access Information**
- Will you be home? Yes/No
- Best time for repair (morning, afternoon, evening, anytime)
- Special access instructions
- Pet information (if applicable)

**Step 6: Submit**
- Review request details
- Click **"Submit Request"**
- Receive confirmation
- Get request tracking number

### Tracking Your Request

**Request Status:**
1. Go to **Maintenance**
2. View all your requests
3. Click on request to see details
4. Status updates:
   - **Pending**: Submitted, awaiting review
   - **Acknowledged**: Landlord has reviewed
   - **In Progress**: Work has started
   - **Completed**: Repair finished
   - **Cancelled**: Request cancelled

**Notifications:**
You'll receive notifications when:
- Landlord acknowledges request
- Vendor is assigned
- Status changes
- Work is completed
- Landlord adds comments

### Communicating About Requests

**Adding Comments:**
1. Open maintenance request
2. Scroll to comments section
3. Add update or question
4. Landlord receives notification
5. View landlord's responses

**Updating Request:**
1. If situation changes, add comment
2. Upload additional photos if needed
3. Update access information if needed

### Emergency Maintenance

**For Emergencies:**
- Gas leak
- No heat in winter
- No water
- Flooding
- Electrical hazard
- Security breach
- Fire or smoke

**Steps:**
1. If life-threatening, call 911 first
2. Contact landlord immediately (use emergency contact number)
3. Submit urgent maintenance request
4. Follow landlord's instructions
5. Document everything with photos

### Maintenance Tips

✅ **Be detailed** - Describe the problem clearly  
✅ **Add photos** - Pictures help landlord understand the issue  
✅ **Set correct priority** - Use urgent only for true emergencies  
✅ **Provide access** - Make it easy for vendors to enter  
✅ **Be patient** - Non-urgent repairs may take a few days  
✅ **Follow up** - Add comments if situation changes  
✅ **Confirm completion** - Let landlord know when work is done

### After Repair is Complete

**Confirming Completion:**
1. Landlord will mark request as completed
2. You'll receive notification
3. Verify repair was done properly
4. If issue persists, reopen request or submit new one
5. Rate the service (optional)

## Messaging Your Landlord

### Sending Messages

**Creating a New Message:**
1. Navigate to **Messages** in the main menu
2. Click **"New Message"**
3. Landlord is automatically selected as recipient
4. Write subject line
5. Compose your message
6. Attach files if needed (documents, photos)
7. Click **"Send"**

**Quick Message:**
1. From anywhere in the app, click the message icon
2. Compose message
3. Send

### Message Threads

**Viewing Conversations:**
- All messages organized by conversation thread
- See full history with your landlord
- Messages sorted by date
- Unread messages highlighted
- Read receipts show when landlord has read your message

### Message Etiquette

**Best Practices:**
✅ **Be professional** - Use respectful, professional language  
✅ **Be clear** - State your purpose clearly  
✅ **Be concise** - Keep messages focused  
✅ **Include details** - Provide relevant information  
✅ **Be patient** - Allow 24-48 hours for response  
✅ **Use proper channel** - Use messages for non-urgent matters

**For Urgent Matters:**
- Use phone call instead of message
- Mark maintenance requests as urgent
- Use emergency contact number for true emergencies

### Attachments

**Sending Files:**
- Click attachment icon
- Select files (up to 10 files, 25MB total)
- Supported formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, JPG, PNG
- Files upload automatically
- Landlord can download attachments

**Common Attachments:**
- Photos of maintenance issues
- Receipts or invoices
- Insurance documents
- Pet vaccination records
- Utility bills
- Other relevant documents

### Message Notifications

**Notification Settings:**
1. Go to **Settings** > **Notifications**
2. Configure message notifications:
   - In-app notifications
   - Email notifications
   - SMS notifications (if enabled)
3. Choose notification frequency
4. Save preferences

**You'll be notified when:**
- New message received
- Landlord reads your message
- Landlord responds to your message

### Message History

**Searching Messages:**
1. Use search bar in Messages
2. Search by keyword, date, or subject
3. Filter by date range
4. View archived messages

**Archiving Messages:**
- Archive old conversations to declutter inbox
- Archived messages can be accessed anytime
- Search includes archived messages

## Tips for Tenants

### Communication

✅ **Respond promptly** - Reply to landlord messages within 24-48 hours  
✅ **Be professional** - Use respectful, professional language  
✅ **Keep records** - Save all communications and documents  
✅ **Ask questions** - Don't hesitate to ask if something is unclear  
✅ **Report issues early** - Don't wait for small problems to become big ones

### Payments

✅ **Pay on time** - Set up auto-pay to never miss a payment  
✅ **Keep receipts** - Save all payment receipts  
✅ **Budget wisely** - Plan for rent and other housing costs  
✅ **Communicate** - If you're having trouble paying, talk to your landlord early  
✅ **Understand fees** - Know your lease terms regarding late fees

### Maintenance

✅ **Report promptly** - Report maintenance issues as soon as you notice them  
✅ **Be detailed** - Provide clear descriptions and photos  
✅ **Provide access** - Make it easy for repairs to be completed  
✅ **Follow up** - Ensure repairs are completed satisfactorily  
✅ **Prevent problems** - Take good care of the property

### Lease

✅ **Read carefully** - Understand all lease terms before signing  
✅ **Follow rules** - Comply with lease terms and property rules  
✅ **Plan ahead** - Think about renewal early  
✅ **Give proper notice** - If moving out, provide required notice  
✅ **Document condition** - Take photos at move-in and move-out

### General

✅ **Keep information updated** - Update contact information promptly  
✅ **Be a good neighbor** - Respect other tenants and property  
✅ **Use the platform** - Take advantage of all available features  
✅ **Stay organized** - Keep all documents and receipts organized  
✅ **Build good relationship** - Maintain positive relationship with landlord
