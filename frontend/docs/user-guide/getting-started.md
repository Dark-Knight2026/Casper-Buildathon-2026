# Getting Started

This guide will help you get started with the Property Management System, covering account creation, login, and basic setup.

## Creating an Account

### Step 1: Navigate to Registration Page

1. Go to the platform homepage
2. Click the **"Sign Up"** button in the top right corner
3. You'll be directed to the registration page

### Step 2: Choose Your Role

Select your account type:
- **Landlord**: For property owners and managers
- **Tenant**: For renters looking for properties

### Step 3: Fill in Your Information

**Required Information:**
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Phone Number
- Password (minimum 8 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character)
- Password Confirmation

**Optional Information:**
- Company Name (for landlords)
- Profile Picture

### Step 4: Verify Your Email

1. After registration, check your email inbox
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. Your account will be activated

**Note**: The verification link expires after 24 hours. If it expires, you can request a new one from the login page.

---

## Logging In

### Standard Login

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click **"Sign In"**

### Forgot Password?

If you forget your password:

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

---

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account.

### Enabling 2FA

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **Settings** > **Security**
3. Click **"Enable Two-Factor Authentication"**
4. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
5. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app
6. Save your backup codes in a secure location

### Using 2FA to Log In

1. Enter your email and password as usual
2. When prompted, open your authenticator app
3. Enter the 6-digit code displayed
4. Click **"Verify"**

### Disabling 2FA

1. Go to **Settings** > **Security**
2. Click **"Disable Two-Factor Authentication"**
3. Enter your password to confirm
4. Enter a 2FA code from your authenticator app

## Profile Settings

### Updating Your Profile

1. Click your profile picture in the top right corner
2. Select **"Profile Settings"**
3. Update your information:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone Number
   - Profile Picture
   - Company Name (landlords only)
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Settings** > **Security**
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password
5. Confirm your new password
6. Click **"Update Password"**

### Notification Preferences

Customize how you receive notifications:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Notifications**
2. Choose your preferences for each notification type:
   - In-App Notifications
   - Email Notifications
   - SMS Notifications (if enabled)
3. Set quiet hours (optional)
4. Choose email digest frequency (daily or weekly)
5. Click **"Save Preferences"**

**Available Notification Types:**
- Payment notifications
- Maintenance request updates
- Lease events
- New messages
- Application updates
- Document notifications
- System announcements

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your dashboard with:

### For Landlords:
- **Quick Stats**: Properties, tenants, active leases, pending maintenance
- **Recent Activity**: Latest payments, maintenance requests, messages
- **Financial Summary**: Revenue, expenses, occupancy rate
- **Upcoming Tasks**: Lease renewals, scheduled maintenance, payment due dates
- **Quick Actions**: Add property, create lease, view reports

### For Tenants:
- **Lease Information**: Current lease details, rent amount, lease end date
- **Payment Status**: Next payment due, payment history
- **Maintenance Requests**: Active and completed requests
- **Messages**: Unread messages from landlord
- **Quick Actions**: Pay rent, submit maintenance request, contact landlord
