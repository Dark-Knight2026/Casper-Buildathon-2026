## 2. Launch Strategy

### 2.1 Launch Timeline

**Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-4)**

**Week 1-2: Beta Testing**
- Recruit 50 beta users (25 landlords, 15 tenants, 10 property managers)
- Conduct user acceptance testing (UAT)
- Gather feedback and iterate
- Fix any critical issues identified

**Week 3-4: Launch Preparation**
- Finalize marketing materials (website, landing pages, videos)
- Set up customer support infrastructure
- Train support team
- Prepare launch communications
- Coordinate with marketing and sales teams

**Phase 2: Soft Launch (Weeks 5-8)**

**Week 5: Limited Launch**
- Open to beta users and early access list (500 users)
- Monitor performance and user feedback closely
- Implement Real User Monitoring (RUM) with Sentry
- Daily check-ins with support team

**Week 6-7: Gradual Expansion**
- Expand to 2,000 users
- Launch referral program
- Begin content marketing (blog posts, case studies)
- Start paid advertising (Google Ads, Facebook Ads)

**Week 8: Full Launch Preparation**
- Analyze soft launch metrics
- Optimize conversion funnel
- Prepare for full launch announcement
- Finalize pricing and packaging

**Phase 3: Full Launch (Week 9+)**

**Week 9: Public Launch**
- Public announcement via press release
- Launch on Product Hunt, Hacker News
- Email marketing campaign to full list
- Social media campaign
- Influencer partnerships

**Week 10-12: Growth & Optimization**
- Scale marketing efforts based on performance
- A/B test landing pages and messaging
- Expand content marketing
- Launch partnership program
- Optimize onboarding flow

**Week 13+: Sustained Growth**
- Continuous feature releases
- Customer success focus
- Expand to new markets (international)
- Enterprise sales outreach

### 4.3 Sales Process

**Self-Service Funnel:**

1. **Awareness**: User discovers platform (ads, content, referral)
2. **Interest**: Visits website, reads content
3. **Consideration**: Signs up for free tier
4. **Trial**: Uses platform, adds properties
5. **Decision**: Upgrades to paid tier
6. **Retention**: Continues using, refers others

**Conversion Optimization:**
- Frictionless sign-up (email + password only)
- Guided onboarding (5-step wizard)
- In-app tooltips and tutorials
- Free tier with real value (not just trial)
- Upgrade prompts at key moments (property #4, advanced feature use)
- Email nurture campaigns
- In-app chat support

**Sales-Assisted Process:**

1. **Lead Generation**: Inbound (demo request) or outbound (cold outreach)
2. **Qualification**: BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
3. **Discovery Call**: Understand needs and pain points (30 min)
4. **Demo**: Customized product demo (45 min)
5. **Proposal**: Custom pricing and contract
6. **Negotiation**: Address concerns, finalize terms
7. **Close**: Sign contract, onboard customer
8. **Implementation**: Setup, training, go-live (2-4 weeks)
9. **Account Management**: Ongoing support and upsells

**Sales Team Structure (Month 6):**
- 1 VP of Sales
- 2 Account Executives (AEs) - enterprise sales
- 3 Sales Development Reps (SDRs) - lead generation
- 1 Sales Operations Manager

**Sales Targets:**
- AEs: $500K ARR per rep annually
- SDRs: 50 qualified leads per month

- Average deal size: $5K-25K annually
