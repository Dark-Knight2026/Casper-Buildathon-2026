## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click "Sign Up" in the top-right corner
2. Enter your email and password
3. Verify your email address
4. Select your role:
   - **Tenant**: Looking for a property to rent
   - **Landlord**: Have properties to rent out
   - **Both**: Both tenant and landlord

### Completing Your Profile

A complete profile helps you get better matches and builds trust.

**For Tenants:**
- Full name
- Phone number
- Profile photo
- Employment status
- Annual income
- Credit score range

**For Landlords:**
- Full name
- Phone number
- Profile photo
- Business information (optional)

## For Tenants

### Step 1: Set Your Preferences

Complete your tenant profile to receive personalized property recommendations:

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** > **Profile**
2. Fill in your preferences:
   - **Budget**: Set your minimum and maximum rent budget
   - **Location**: Add preferred cities or neighborhoods
   - **Property Type**: Apartment, house, condo, etc.
   - **Bedrooms & Bathrooms**: Minimum requirements
   - **Amenities**: Select must-have amenities (parking, gym, etc.)
   - **Lease Term**: Preferred lease duration
   - **Move-in Date**: When you need to move in
   - **Pets**: Indicate if you have pets and what type

3. Click **Save Preferences**

### Step 2: Browse Properties

#### View Recommendations

Your dashboard shows personalized property recommendations based on your preferences:

- **Match Score**: See how well each property matches your preferences (0-100%)
- **Match Breakdown**: Understand why it's a good match
  - Price compatibility
  - Location proximity
  - Amenity match
  - Lease term alignment
  - Pet policy compatibility

#### Search Properties

Use the search and filter tools:

1. **Search Bar**: Enter location, property type, or keywords
2. **Filters**:
   - Price range slider
   - Location with radius
   - Property type checkboxes
   - Bedrooms/bathrooms
   - Amenities
   - Lease terms
   - Pet policy
   - Furnished/unfurnished
   - Available date

3. **Sort By**:
   - Best match
   - Price (low to high)
   - Price (high to low)
   - Newest listings
   - Most viewed

#### View Property Details

Click on any property card to see:

- **Photo Gallery**: Swipe through property images
- **Property Details**: Full description, specs, and features
- **Amenities**: Complete list of included amenities
- **Location Map**: See exact location and nearby places
- **Landlord Profile**: View landlord information and ratings
- **Match Score**: Detailed breakdown of your compatibility
- **Similar Properties**: Other properties you might like

### Step 3: Save Favorites

Click the heart icon on any property to save it to your favorites:

- Access favorites from **Dashboard** > **Saved Properties**
- Remove from favorites by clicking the heart again
- Receive notifications if saved properties update

### Step 4: Submit Application

When you find the perfect property:

1. Click **Apply Now** on the property details page
2. Complete the application form:
   - **Personal Information**: Name, contact, DOB
   - **Employment Details**: Employer, position, income
   - **Rental History**: Previous addresses and landlords
   - **References**: Personal and professional references
   - **Documents**: Upload ID, pay stubs, bank statements

3. Review and submit

**Application Tips:**
- Complete all required fields
- Upload clear, legible documents
- Provide accurate information
- Include strong references
- Write a brief introduction about yourself

### Step 5: Track Application Status

Monitor your applications in **Dashboard** > **Applications**:

- **Pending**: Application submitted, awaiting review
- **Under Review**: Landlord is reviewing your application
- **Screening**: Background check in progress
- **Approved**: Congratulations! You're approved
- **Rejected**: Application was not accepted
- **Withdrawn**: You withdrew your application

### Step 6: Communicate with Landlords

Use the built-in messaging system:

1. Click **Contact Landlord** on any property
2. Send a message introducing yourself
3. Ask questions about the property
4. Schedule property viewings
5. Discuss lease terms

**Messaging Tips:**
- Be professional and courteous
- Respond promptly to messages
- Ask specific questions
- Be honest about your situation
- Keep all communication on the platform

### Step 7: Complete Lease

Once approved:

1. Review lease terms carefully
2. Ask questions about anything unclear
3. Sign lease electronically
4. Pay security deposit and first month's rent
5. Schedule move-in inspection
6. Receive keys and move in!

## For Landlords

### Step 1: List Your Property

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** > **Properties** > **Add Property**
2. Fill in property details:
   - **Basic Info**: Title, description, property type
   - **Location**: Full address
   - **Specifications**: Bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage
   - **Pricing**: Monthly rent, security deposit
   - **Availability**: Available date, lease terms
   - **Amenities**: Select all applicable amenities
   - **Pet Policy**: Specify pet rules
   - **Photos**: Upload high-quality images (minimum 5)

3. Click **Publish Listing**

**Listing Tips:**
- Use clear, high-quality photos
- Write detailed descriptions
- Highlight unique features
- Be accurate about amenities
- Set competitive pricing
- Update availability promptly

### Step 2: View Tenant Matches

The system automatically matches your property with qualified tenants:

1. Go to **Dashboard** > **Matches**
2. View tenant profiles sorted by match score
3. See compatibility breakdown:
   - Budget compatibility
   - Preference alignment
   - Lease term match
   - Pet policy compatibility

4. Click on tenant profiles to learn more:
   - Employment status
   - Income level
   - Credit score range
   - Rental history
   - References

### Step 3: Review Applications

When tenants apply to your property:

1. Receive notification of new application
2. Go to **Dashboard** > **Applications**
3. Review application details:
   - Personal information
   - Employment and income
   - Rental history
   - References
   - Uploaded documents

4. Request screening report (if not already done)
5. Review screening results:
   - Credit report
   - Criminal background check
   - Eviction history
   - Income verification

### Step 4: Manage Applications

For each application, you can:

- **Approve**: Accept the tenant
- **Reject**: Decline the application (provide reason)
- **Request More Info**: Ask for additional documents
- **Schedule Viewing**: Arrange property tour
- **Message Applicant**: Communicate directly

**Application Review Tips:**
- Review all applications fairly
- Follow Fair Housing laws
- Verify all information
- Check references
- Trust your screening reports
- Respond promptly to applicants

### Step 5: Communicate with Tenants

Use the messaging system to:

- Answer questions about the property
- Schedule property viewings
- Request additional information
- Discuss lease terms
- Coordinate move-in details

### Step 6: Finalize Lease

Once you approve a tenant:

1. Send lease agreement through the platform
2. Review and negotiate terms if needed
3. Collect security deposit and first month's rent
4. Schedule move-in inspection
5. Provide keys and access codes
6. Welcome your new tenant!

### Step 7: Manage Properties

From your dashboard:

- **Edit Listings**: Update property details anytime
- **Mark as Rented**: Update status when property is occupied
- **View Analytics**: See listing views, inquiries, applications
- **Manage Multiple Properties**: Handle entire portfolio
- **Track Payments**: Monitor rent collection (if applicable)

3. Type your message

- **Property Context**: Messages linked to specific properties
- **Search**: Find past conversations easily

### Best Practices

- **Be Professional**: Keep communication courteous
- **Respond Promptly**: Reply within 24 hours
- **Be Clear**: Communicate clearly and concisely
- **Keep Records**: All messages are saved for reference
- **Stay On Platform**: Keep all communication in the app

## Notifications

### Notification Types

You'll receive notifications for:

- **New Matches**: When properties match your preferences
- **Application Updates**: Status changes on your applications
- **New Applications**: When tenants apply to your property
- **New Messages**: When you receive a message
- **Payment Reminders**: Upcoming payment due dates
- **Review Requests**: When someone asks for a review

### Notification Channels

- **In-App**: See notifications in the notification bell
- **Email**: Receive email notifications (configurable)
- **Push**: Browser push notifications (opt-in)
- **SMS**: Text message alerts (optional, premium feature)

### Managing Notifications

1. Go to **Settings** > **Notifications**
2. Choose which notifications you want to receive
3. Select notification channels for each type
4. Set quiet hours (no notifications during specified times)
5. Save preferences

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is the platform free to use?**
A: Basic features are free. Premium features may require a subscription.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Note: This is permanent.

**Q: Can I use the platform on mobile?**
A: Yes! The platform is fully responsive and works on all devices.

### For Tenants

**Q: How are match scores calculated?**
A: Match scores consider price compatibility (30%), location (25%), amenities (20%), lease terms (15%), and pet policy (10%).

**Q: Can I apply to multiple properties?**
A: Yes, you can submit applications to as many properties as you like.

**Q: What documents do I need to apply?**
A: Typically: ID, proof of income (pay stubs), bank statements, and references.

**Q: How long does screening take?**
A: Background checks usually complete within 24-48 hours.

**Q: What if my application is rejected?**
A: You can apply to other properties. Landlords may provide feedback.

### For Landlords

**Q: How much does it cost to list a property?**
A: Basic listings are free. Premium features are available with subscription.

**Q: How do I screen tenants?**
A: Screening reports are available for $35 per applicant (can be charged to tenant).

**Q: Can I list multiple properties?**
A: Yes, there's no limit to the number of properties you can list.

**Q: What if I need to remove a listing?**
A: You can mark properties as inactive or delete them anytime.

**Q: How do I handle security deposits?**
A: Security deposits can be collected through the platform or offline.

### Technical Support

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset it.

**Q: My messages aren't sending. Help!**
A: Check your internet connection. If the problem persists, contact support.

**Q: How do I report a problem?**
A: Click "Help" > "Contact Support" or email support@marketplace.com

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices.

## Safety Tips

### For Tenants

- ✅ Verify property listings are legitimate
- ✅ Meet landlords in person before signing
- ✅ Inspect property thoroughly before moving in
- ✅ Read lease carefully before signing
- ✅ Keep all communication on the platform
- ❌ Never wire money without seeing the property
- ❌ Don't share sensitive financial info via message
- ❌ Avoid landlords who pressure you to decide quickly

### For Landlords

- ✅ Screen all applicants thoroughly
- ✅ Verify employment and income
- ✅ Check references carefully
- ✅ Follow Fair Housing laws
- ✅ Document property condition with photos
- ❌ Don't discriminate based on protected classes
- ❌ Don't skip background checks
- ❌ Avoid accepting cash payments without receipts
